What is this?

Every day, we look at every tweet sent by U.S. federal government Twitter accounts and rank them based on an algorithm that looks at audience size, retweets, and favorites. Great Gov Tweets highlights the amazing work Government communicators do every day.

Are you tracking my Twitter account?

We look at all Twitter accounts in USA.gov’s Social Media Registry. If you manage a U.S. federal government Twitter account, look it up in the registry on HowTo.gov. If it’s not there, add it.
